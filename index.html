About Me

As a child, I frequently bombarded my grandparents with questions that they were not able to answer. To me, everything was and still is a mystery and I was and still am the detective on a journey to learn about all of the unknowns in my life.

This sense of curiosity led me to study computer science in high school. Learning how to code has taught me to think differently. Getting the codes to work requires trial and error. Like solving a mystery, we don’t always get the murderer correctly on our first try. New evidence will surface and the suspects will find alibis, but we must carry on until we logically figure out the correct answer. I got fascinated by how much technology revolutionized different industries, therefore, I decided to pursue computer science at the University of Pittsburgh. In college, I took several courses and participated in many activities that strongly increased my interest and passion for web development, data science, and machine learning. Every day I look forward to overcoming different challenges.

The experience of living under two distinct cultures brought me a sensitivity and a strong interest in learning languages and allowed me to quickly adapt to changes and gain a broader perspective. Thus, when I started learning to code, my experience helped me learn it quickly that further allows me to build lasting connections, developing my professional acumen, and preparing myself for a possible future career. I am looking forward to implementing and polishing them in my career.

The environment of the University of Pittsburgh fancies me a lot. The Gothic building is a bit like Hogwarts in Harry Potter. In addition to its amazing buildings, I feel Pittsburgh can help me succeed as it values experiential learning and launch very successful careers through internships, and Co-op programs.

What is the work environment that you thrive in the most?

I’ve thrived in more flexible and collaborative environments. I enjoy working as part of a team, even though I can do independent work, too. I really like working with people who are committed to getting things done and who are passionate about their work. Also, I like to get feedback from others to improve myself.

What makes a job right for you?
